Renewable vs coal and gas.

To illustrate the potential effect on greenhouse gas emissions we ask the following question: what are the associated emissions if the electricity generated from the investment would have been generated by a mix of fossil fuels? The calculations are based on the emissions factors provided by IPCC (in grams CO2eq. /kWh). The results are based on life-cycle emissions generated by the power plants funded by the investor..
